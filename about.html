About Me

Hi, I’m Nadia. I’ve been coding since my dad gave me his Sinclair ZX-81 and I learned BASIC. I studied Philosophy and Artificial Intelligence at University, and workeed at radio station as an engineer and DJ while I was there. Since then, I’ve worked in telecommunications, video, healthcare, and aviation.

After building my first Skyrim mod, I realized my time could be better spent learning to make games myself. After debating the pros and cons of each, I decided to learn Unity3D. I already knew C#, so it was an easier transition than learning C++ as well as Unreal.
